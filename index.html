What we optimize for (UK visa photo)

The app guides composition toward the digital photo spec published on gov.uk. Always confirm the latest rules before you submit — government guidance can change without notice.

  • 630×810 px export — comfortably exceeds gov.uk's 600×750 px minimum, at the correct 35:45 aspect ratio.
  • Light grey/cream background — not white — matching gov.uk's stated rule.
  • Head height 64–76% of frame (29–34mm of a 45mm frame), the print-photo convention we also apply to the digital export.
  • JPEG size policy — export sized within gov.uk's 50 KB – 6 MB window.
  • No face reshaping — segmentation and cropping only; we don't slim or resize facial geometry.
  • Privacy — segmentation and matting run on your iPhone; no separate upload to our servers for processing.

This app targets the digital upload spec used for online visa/UKVI account applications. A printed 35×45mm photo (same physical size) is still used as a fallback at some VFS Global visa application centres — the same export works for both. Final acceptance is always at the discretion of UKVI or the visa application center you use.
